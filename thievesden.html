Thieves' Den

Thieves' Den is mainly to preserve all grassy lands in the Inpherno and is one of the 4 major reigions. They have large structures and cliffs as its terrain. It's fauna (animal life in the region) is mainly carnivorous plants which in this case are inphernal-eating plants.
